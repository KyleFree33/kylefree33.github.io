The opposite of an echo chamber.

Well thought out ideas are upvoted regardless of opinion. Healthy disagreement is encouraged - the best way to learn is to be challenged.

It is exciting when someone disagrees with you:

  • If you find out you're wrong, you learn something new.
  • If a good argument for the other side is given and you still disagree, you learn more about your own position and are prepared to defend it in the future.

Remember:

Ideas are not owned; you want the best ones to serve you.

Ideas are treated as machines, and we aim for the best ones.

Upvote topics that have good points and make you think, regardless of your personal opinions.
